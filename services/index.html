One Quote, One Project Manager, One Warranty

The Florida Florida Energy Code, the EPA RRP lead-safe rule for pre-1978 homes, the Florida Energy Heat Pump Contractor program, the new 2024 ADU law — every one of these adds compliance work that single-trade contractors miss. We hold the licenses, file the permits, run the rebate paperwork, and coordinate the trades so your project doesn't bounce between five different companies. The same Lead General Contractor who walks your kitchen remodel also coordinates the electrical panel upgrade your kitchen triggered, the Florida Energy heat pump install your homeowner's-association overlay required, and the EPA RRP containment your pre-1978 plaster wall demanded.
